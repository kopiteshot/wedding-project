# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Wedding guests, friends, and family of Hoài Anh and Quang Đại. They are visiting the site to see wedding details, the couple's story, photo gallery, and to RSVP.

## Product Purpose
To serve as a beautiful, premium digital wedding invitation that conveys the couple's story and provides all necessary logistical details for their special day.

## Brand Personality
Romantic, elegant, warm, refined, and cinematic.

## Anti-references
Generic wedding templates with overly complex particle effects or dated bounce animations.

## Design Principles
- Show, don't tell: Let the photography and typography lead the visual hierarchy.
- Cinematic reveals: Use high-quality, smooth motion that feels orchestrated rather than reactive.
- Graceful degradation: Ensure the experience remains beautiful even with reduced motion.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Respects `prefers-reduced-motion` for all scroll reveals and animations.
- Ensure high contrast for text over backgrounds.
